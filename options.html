How it works

  • Every shot is automatically saved in the browser — no clicking needed

  • Select your club in the overlay header — it's the first column in the export
  • Landing dots show your last 100 carry distances per club on the 3D range

AI Shot Coaching (DigitalOcean)

  • Sends each shot to a DigitalOcean GenAI endpoint for a quick summary + coaching tip

  • Your API key is stored locally in this browser only
